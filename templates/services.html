Services

MediLab offers advanced diagnostics and comprehensive medical services with a focus on personalized care and innovation.

Nutritional Counselling

Medilab provides personalized nutritional counseling to promote wellness and manage chronic conditions through diet and lifestyle modifications, enhancing overall health outcomes.

Pediatric Services

Medilab offers specialized pediatric services including developmental assessments, vaccinations, and blood tests tailored for children's health and well-being.

Women's Health

Dedicated services for women's health include mammograms, Pap smears, and fertility testing, ensuring comprehensive care tailored to women's specific health needs.

Cardiac Screening

Medilab provides cardiac screening services such as ECGs and stress tests to evaluate heart health and detect cardiovascular conditions early.

Laboratory Testing

Comprehensive laboratory testing services at Medilab encompass blood tests, urine analysis, and genetic testing, aiding in disease diagnosis and treatment planning.

Diagnostic Imaging

Medilab offers state-of-the-art diagnostic imaging services including X-rays, CT scans, and MRI scans to accurately visualize internal structures and detect abnormalities.
